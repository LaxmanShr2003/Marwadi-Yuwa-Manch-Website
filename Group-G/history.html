Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much. History of MYM,Nepal

The youths of Marwadi Sewa Samiti, with pure intention of social works formed Marwadi Yuva Manch,Nepal.

It was inaugurated by one of the main leaders of Nepali Congress & a former Cabinet Minister Shri Ganesh Man Singh on 03 Jyestha 2046 BS at Marwadi Sewa Samiti’s head office. The inauguration was led by Shri Mohan Gopal Khetan.

The youths of Marwadi Yuva Munch agreed upon to form a 25 members of Board of Directors.

Our Project

Marwadi Yuwa Manch is dedicated to promoting community well-being through a comprehensive range of health-focused initiatives. Our primary objective is to improve the health and vitality of individuals by organizing and facilitating various health programs. These programs include blood donation drives, free health check-ups, and other health awareness campaigns. By actively engaging with the community, we strive to enhance health awareness, encourage regular health screenings, and foster a culture of proactive well-being. Through these efforts, we aim to make a meaningful contribution to the overall health and quality of life of individuals while fostering a sense of social responsibility and unity.

Marwadi Sewa Samiti, Nepal

Marwadi Sewa Samiti Nepal (MSSN) is a social organization established in 2010 B.S. [1953 AD]. Since its inception, MSSN is focused on serving society through different initiatives. Currently, over 5000 marwadis comprised of Nepali nationals as well as a great number of Indian diaspora are the members of MSSN and MSSN has many sub wings to serve every segment of society as well as to further promote the people-to-people ties between the people of Nepal and India in grassroots Nepal. We at MSSN believe that “ सेवा हि परमो धर्म “. Our gaushala is functional since 1940 A.D (1997 B.S) and we are running prosthetic leg distribution programs and more than 500 needy people are already benefited from this initiative. At Pasupathi Gaushala we are also running a 30-bed hemodialysis center.
