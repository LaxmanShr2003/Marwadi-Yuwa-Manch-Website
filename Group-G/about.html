Who Are We

MYM is an elected body of the Youth members below 45 yrs working as a youth wing of MSSN, Nepal.

Marwadi Yuva Manch (MYM), Nepal is a youth wing of Marwadi Sewa Samiti, Nepal (MSSN). Marwadi Yuva Manch was established in 2049 B.S. (1992 A.D). MYM is a voluntary youth organization and any member (aged below 45 years) of Marwadi Sewa Samiti is a member of MYM by default. MSSN

MYM's Objective

When the youth is empowered, a society is empowered and when society is empowered, a nation is empowered. The basic objective of MYM is to empower the youths of the Marwadi Community to help in building a better society and a better nation. The MYM has a proven track record of social service since 1992 in the education & health sectors through various programmes. In these 30 years, MYM has organized many cultural programs to unite the members of society. MYM knows that youth is not just about enjoyment and merriment but also about becoming responsible and wise.
