Who Are We

MYM is an elected body of the Youth members below 45 yrs working as a youth wing of MSSN, Nepal.

Marwadi Yuva Manch (MYM), Nepal is a youth wing of Marwadi Sewa Samiti, Nepal (MSSN). Marwadi Yuva Manch was established in 2049 B.S. (1992 A.D). MYM is a voluntary youth organization and any member (aged below 45 years) of Marwadi Sewa Samiti is a member of MYM by default. MSSN

Certified and Award Winning Service Provider

Marwadi Youth Movement (MYM) proudly stands as a Certified and Award-Winning Service Provider. With a legacy rooted in over three decades of dedicated service to the Marwadi Community and beyond, MYM has earned its reputation through unwavering commitment, impactful initiatives, and transformative contributions. Through its diverse array of programs, ranging from educational and healthcare endeavors to cultural enrichment and community unity initiatives, MYM has consistently demonstrated excellence in service delivery. The accolades and recognition garnered by MYM not only reflect its dedication to uplifting society but also highlight its role as a beacon of positive change, making a meaningful difference in the lives of individuals and the broader community.
